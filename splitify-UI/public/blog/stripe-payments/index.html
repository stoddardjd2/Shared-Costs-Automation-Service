Payments with Stripe: Safety, Speed, and Simplicity

The industry-standard rails for Splitify payments

By Splitify Team·February 8, 2025·7 min read

Stripe powers our checkout and payouts, bringing modern security and reliable infrastructure to Splitify payments. In plain English: it's safe, it's fast, and it just works.

Secure by default

Stripe tokenizes payment details so sensitive information never touches Splitify servers. Features like PCI-compliant handling, 3D Secure, and built-in fraud detection keep transactions safe.

Fast and flexible

Support for major cards and bank payments where available, with settlement that helps reimbursements land without long delays.

Great checkout experience

Polished, mobile-friendly checkout reduces friction and increases completed payments. Your friends can pay from their phones in seconds.

What it means for you

  • Clear receipts for everyone involved.
  • Refunds and adjustments handled cleanly through the same flow.
  • Consistent performance backed by industry-standard infrastructure.

Payments should be the easy part. With Splitify and Stripe, they are.
