Case Study: Roommates Splitting Utilities in 60 Seconds

Real-world speed, less awkwardness

By Splitify Team·February 22, 2025·6 min read

Meet Alex, Priya, Jonah, and Mei. Their $154.00 internet bill just landed. Here's how they split and paid it in under a minute without a single "hey did you see my message?" in the group chat.

Set up (20 seconds)

Alex opens Splitify, creates a new cost called "Internet — March," selects equal split for four people, and sets the due date to Friday. He adds a short note: "Thanks all!"

Send (10 seconds)

Alex taps send. Splitify delivers clear requests with each person's exact amount ($38.50), the due date, and a secure payment link.

Gentle reminders (automatic)

Two people pay right away. The others get a polite reminder the day before it's due. No nagging, no awkwardness.

Done (the next day)

Within 24 hours, all four payments are in. The dashboard shows "Paid" across the board, and receipts are saved automatically.

What made it smooth

  • Everyone saw their exact amount and due date up front.
  • Paying took seconds on mobile.
  • Reminders were spaced and polite (and stopped as soon as someone paid).
